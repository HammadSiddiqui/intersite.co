Unilever WPS Portal

The Workplace and Travel Services (WPS) portal is a new app for the employees working at unilever to request for several types of services available at Unilever.

  • Users can request services in just 3 taps
  • All requests are immediately notified to the resolvers
  • Resolvers can fullfil all request from the resolver's dasboard
  • Admins can keep an eye on the entire process from the admin dashboard
  • Real-time Analytics and Reports generation
